Tentative Business Rules & Restrictions:

1. Users must be registered to access the application 
2. Each application must be linked to a company (users cannot log an application without selecting a company) 
3. Application status must be one of the predefined values: "Applied", "Interview Scheduled", "Offer Recieved", "Rejected" or "Withdrawn" 
4. A job application can have multiple interviews, but each interview must be associated with only one application.
5. A company can have multiple job postings, but each job posting belongs to one company 
6. Users can add notes to applications, but they must be associated with an existing application. 
7. Users may store documents, such as resumes and cover letters, but each document must be linked to a specific job application.
